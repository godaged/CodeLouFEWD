W E L C O M E
to Louisville City

Louisville in Kentucky, a city that has something for everyone. If you’re coming with your children, check out the Kentucky Science Center, the Louisville Zoo, or Louisville’s Waterfront Park. All of these activities are sure to keep your kids having a ton of fun! If you’re coming with your spouse or significant other, there is even more things to do in Louisville. Check out great brunch spots in NuLu or the Highlands, watch a race at Churchill Downs, or grab a drink along the Bourbon Trail. If you’re the adventurous type, try a tour of Louisville’s Mega Caverns, which includes a zip line course and underground bike course. No matter who you are coming to Louisville with, there’s a fun time to be had by everyone!
